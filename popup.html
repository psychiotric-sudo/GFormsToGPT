🔒 Privacy Policy
Your privacy is our top priority. We collect zero personal information.
What We Track
To improve our service, we anonymously track:
• Installation events: When you first install
• Form fill count: How many forms you've filled

All tracking uses a random User ID (format: User-XXXX) with no link to your identity.
❌ We Never Collect
• Your name, email, or any personal data
• URLs of forms you visit
• Form content or your answers
• Your IP address or browsing history
• Any ChatGPT account information
🔐 How Data Is Sent
Anonymous usage data is sent only to Discord webhooks for monitoring purposes. These webhooks are secure and encrypted.
💡 Your Control
You have full control. The extension runs entirely on your device. You can inspect our open-source code at any time to verify we're telling the truth.
